About

MCBEDL is a small project that provides easy access to Minecraft APK releases for Android devices.

This website was created for players who want to try and experience the game, especially those who may not currently have the budget to purchase it.

If you enjoy the game, we encourage you to support the developers by purchasing the official version on the Google Play Store. Buying the original version helps support Mojang Studios and the continued development of Minecraft.

All APK download links on this website are fetched directly from the GitHub repository to keep them updated and accessible. The files are provided as-is with no encryption or hidden content.

If there are any issues or concerns related to this website, the content will be reviewed and removed if necessary.
