About

MCBEDL is a small project that provides free access to Minecraft APK releases for Android devices.

This website is mainly intended for players who want to test the game, create mods, addons, or resource packs.

All APK files are fetched directly from the GitHub repository to keep them updated and accessible. Files are provided as-is with no encryption or hidden content.

If you enjoy the game, please consider purchasing the official version on the Google Play Store to support Mojang Studios and the continued development of Minecraft.

Credits: 0x4a4b, Zihao_il, ChatGPT, and everyone who supports this project.
